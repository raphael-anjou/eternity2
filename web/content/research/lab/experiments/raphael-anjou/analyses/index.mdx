These are not attempts on the score. They answer questions the
[runs](/research/lab/experiments/raphael-anjou/runs) raise but cannot settle:
what the true best ending of a band actually is, and what the community's best
boards did that a normal solver would never do.

They earn no row on the score chart, which is the point: an exact answer about
a small region is worth more than another near-miss on the whole board.
