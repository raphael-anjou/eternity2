Every page here is one search run with a name, an idea, and a board. They share
the [engines](/research/lab/experiments/raphael-anjou/engines); what differs is
how each one steers them: what it seeds, what it forbids, what it tears up and
rebuilds.

They are ordered by what they taught, not by score. A run that ended lower but
explained why is worth more here than one that scraped a point and could not say
how.
