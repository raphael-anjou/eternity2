For the people who want to actually solve it. Pick a door: why the puzzle is
so hard, what you need to build a solver, or the lab: an open notebook of
original findings and experiments, each credited to the researcher who ran it.

  </Door>
  <Door to="/research/build" title="Build a solver">

reduction, exact methods, hardware) from the [topics page](/research/topics).
