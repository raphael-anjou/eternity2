# Digest 0008 — 2009-09 → 2010-02
Coverage: 701 messages, msg_num 6962–7662.

Window context: this window spans the second scrutiny date (2009-12-31). The
headline arc: a quieter, tool-building autumn (E2Lab is born, benchmarks get
cross-verified) → an unverified 471 claim rattles the list in December →
the scrutiny date passes in silence → on 2010-01-18 the official site posts
"Eternity II remains unsolved" with NO winner and NO new best-partial prize →
david.hollis extracts a written Q&A from Tomy confirming the $10k was a
year-one-only promotion and that adjudicators only checked claims of complete
solutions in 2009. The 467 record therefore stands untouched. Bonus primary
source: Louis Verhaard states in his own words that his wife submitted his
best solution and won the $10,000.
