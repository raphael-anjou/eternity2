### 2007-08-18/22 — Brendan derives E2's design parameters ("hardest puzzle" theory)
- who: Brendan Owen; Eternity Two (Brendan's moderator account)
- what: Brendan lays out the hardest-puzzle recipe: compact shape, no symmetric/duplicate pieces, separate border/interior edge types, flat distribution, ~1 expected solution — and derives I = (196!·4^196)^(1/392) ≈ 17.14 → 17 interior colours, 5 border colours, exactly E2's parameters. He states the 17+5 split "were worked out purely mathematically to try and even out the tileability and give an expected solution of about 1", backs it with simulations, and computes I=17,B=5 as >100x harder than I=16,B=8. A follow-up table gives hardest-parameter estimates for all board sizes.
- source: msg_num 1947 — https://groups.io/g/eternity2/message/1947 — "Design the hardest puzzle"; msg_num 1962 — https://groups.io/g/eternity2/message/1962 — "Design the hardest puzzle"; msg_num 2164 — https://groups.io/g/eternity2/message/2164 — "Hardest Puzzle Estimates"

### 2007-08-21 — Border edge-type balance stated (12 of each type per side)
- who: angwin_uk (David); mjqxxxx (Mike)
